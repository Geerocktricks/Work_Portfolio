About me

Hello, my name is Gerald Waweru, I'm 24 years old. I'm a tech enthusiast, really fascinated about design and the broader aspect of technology. I taught myself the basics of design while in campus and since then, I fell in love with the idea of understanding and creating applications that not only offer great User experience, but also applications that are driven towards problem solving. I have had experience in developing various applications for over 2 years now. Prior to which I have done a certificate in full-stack development to back me up. I have a good understanding on computer science fundamentals, and I am constantly improving my skills as a developer. I strongly believe that team work is what makes a developer stand out and grow. When am not coding, I love playing the guitar and playing video games.
